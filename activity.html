Activities of AEROmission AEROmission conducts a wide range of technical and educational activities designed to provide practical exposure and industry-relevant skills in the field of aerospace and aviation. 1. Aerospace Workshops Hands-on workshops covering the fundamentals of aeronautics, aerospace engineering, and aviation technology, conducted by experienced professionals and mentors. 2. Aircraft Model Design & Fabrication Design and build fixed-wing aircraft models to understand aerodynamics, structure, and flight stability through real-time experimentation. 3. Drone Technology Training Training programs on drone design, assembly, flight control systems, and applications in surveillance, mapping, and logistics. 4. Flight Principles & Aerodynamics Sessions Interactive sessions explaining lift, drag, thrust, stability, and control with practical demonstrations and simulations. 5. Simulation-Based Learning Exposure to flight simulators and aerospace design software to enhance analytical and design skills. 6. Student Projects & Research Support Guidance and mentoring for academic projects, mini projects, and research activities related to aerospace and aviation. 7. Competitions & Challenges Organization of aero-design challenges, drone competitions, and innovation contests to encourage creativity and teamwork. 8. Industry Interaction & Expert Talks Guest lectures and webinars by aerospace engineers, pilots, and industry experts to provide real-world insights. 9. Career Guidance & Skill Development Career counseling sessions, roadmap planning, and skill development programs for aerospace and aviation careers. 10. Internship & Training Programs Short-term and long-term internship opportunities to help students gain industry exposure and practical experience.
